European Security Testing.
Built Here. Stays Here.

GetHacked.eu is a security testing platform built in the EU, hosted in the EU, and governed by EU law. We exist because European businesses deserve a security partner that shares their values on data protection and sovereignty.

Our Mission

Security testing generates some of the most sensitive data an organisation can produce — a detailed map of every vulnerability. That data deserves the strongest possible protection.

We built GetHacked.eu because too many European businesses were forced to send their vulnerability data to providers outside the EU, exposing it to foreign jurisdiction risks they could not control.

Our mission is simple: deliver professional-grade penetration testing and vulnerability scanning that keeps every byte of data within EU borders, under EU law, without compromise.

Our Principles
  • Data sovereignty is non-negotiable. All infrastructure is EU-hosted. No data leaves EU borders. No exceptions.
  • We practice what we preach. Our own platform is built to the same security standard we test others against.
  • Transparency over FUD. Clear pricing, honest communication, no scare tactics.
  • Offence drives defence. Real attackers do not follow checklists. We find vulnerabilities the way adversaries do — through creative, hands-on exploitation.

Why EU Data Sovereignty Matters

No CLOUD Act Exposure

We are an EU-registered company. Unlike US-headquartered providers, we cannot be compelled by the CLOUD Act, FISA, or Executive Order 12333 to hand over your data to foreign governments.

Your Data Stays Locked Down

End-to-end encryption, per-client data isolation, and strict access controls. Your pentest findings are treated like the crown jewels they are.

EU Infrastructure Only

All processing and storage in data centres located in Germany and the Netherlands. No Schrems II concerns because no cross-border transfer occurs.

Built Secure, Not Just Selling Security

A security company's own platform must be exemplary. Here is how we hold ourselves accountable.

Strict Tenant Isolation

Every query is scoped to the authenticated user's organisation and role. Client A cannot see Client B's data. Our architecture supports fully isolated per-client environments for enterprise customers.

Least Privilege by Design

Three distinct roles (Client, Pentester, Administrator) with precisely scoped permissions. Pentesters see only their assigned engagements. No role can exceed its intended access boundaries.

Battle-Tested by Our Own Team

We pentest our own platform. Injection, broken access control, cross-tenant data leakage, privilege escalation — if we find it in your apps, we've already hunted it in ours.

Complete Audit Trail

Every action is logged: scope submissions, offer creation, finding additions, report downloads. Full accountability for clients, pentesters, and administrators.
