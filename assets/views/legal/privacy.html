Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

GetHacked.eu is a security testing platform. We aim to process all data within the European Union.

2. What Data We Collect

Account Data

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address as provided through our identity provider. Authentication is handled via OpenID Connect — we do not store passwords.

Service Data

When you use our services, we process the targets you define (domains, IP addresses) and store the resulting scan data, findings, and reports.

Technical Data

We collect server access logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) for security monitoring and abuse prevention.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide our security testing services
  • To authenticate your identity and manage access
  • To generate reports and findings
  • To communicate with you about your engagements
  • To monitor our platform for security issues

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data Storage

We use EU-based infrastructure for data storage. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Each client's data is separated through application-level access controls.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your account plus a reasonable period after closure. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting hello@gethacked.eu. Deletion requests are subject to any legal retention obligations.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at hello@gethacked.eu.
