Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

GetHacked.eu is operated by a European Union-registered company. All data processing occurs exclusively within the EU. We are subject only to EU law and cannot be compelled by foreign jurisdictions to disclose your data.

2. What Data We Collect

Account Data

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and organisation details as provided through our identity provider. Authentication is handled via OpenID Connect — we never store passwords.

Service Data

When you use our scanning or penetration testing services, we process the targets you define (domains, IP addresses, URLs) and store the resulting vulnerability findings, reports, and engagement records.

Technical Data

We collect server access logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) for security monitoring and abuse prevention. These logs are retained for 90 days.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our security testing services
  • To authenticate your identity and enforce access controls
  • To generate vulnerability reports and findings
  • To communicate with you about your engagements
  • To monitor our platform for security threats

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in EU-based data centres located in Germany and the Netherlands. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Each client's data is logically isolated through strict tenant separation.

5. Data Retention

Engagement data and findings are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting security@gethacked.eu.

6. Your Rights

Under EU data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at security@gethacked.eu.
