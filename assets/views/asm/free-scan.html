Free Scan FAQ

What data do you collect during the free scan?

We perform passive reconnaissance only — DNS lookups, certificate transparency log queries, and non-intrusive port checks. We do not store your domain or results after the scan completes. No account is created.

Is this scan safe? Will it trigger alerts on my infrastructure?

Yes, it is safe. We use passive techniques that do not send exploit payloads or modify anything on your systems. The scan is comparable to what search engines and certificate monitors already do.

How is this different from paid ASM monitoring?

The free scan is a one-time snapshot. Paid continuous monitoring runs 24/7, detects changes as they happen, sends alerts, tracks shadow IT over time, and includes API access and integrations.

Can I scan any domain?

You may scan domains you own or have explicit authorisation to test. We verify domain ownership for paid plans. The free scan uses only publicly available data.

Where does the scan run?

All scanning infrastructure is hosted in the European Union. Data never leaves EU borders. No third-party services outside the EU are involved.
